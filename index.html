My Journey

The Path that led me to data.

  • 2012-2015

    Bachelor of Sports Science.

    After Highschool I completed a Sports Science Degree nad begun work as a Personal Trainer. Developing my skills in communication and presentation.

  • 2015-2018

    Developing presentation skills.

    Whilst working as a PT and Team Trainer, My confidence in presenting and promoting services increased, Building the skills needed to entice and capture a wide range of audiences.

  • Dec 2018-Mar 2019

    Data Science Immersive.

    The first steps to my new career path, Learning Python, SQL and other tools within 12 weeks, and rediscovering a love for problemsolving and working with numbers.

  • March 2019

    Capitalising on my strengths.

    Throughout the General Assembly Course, my presentations set me apart from the others in my cohort, bringing the confidence learnt in the fitness industry and the ability to interpret for both technical and non technical audiences, I found the joy of finding the story behind the data and being able to present it effectively.
